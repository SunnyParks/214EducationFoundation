Site Wide Changes

...Website Footer

The footer of the website underwent several changes to call out the most important features of the site. The new footer features interactive buttons, that turn red when the user hovers over them. The format was also modified to accentuate the foundation and its various social platforms.


...Before ................................................ After














...Site Menu









The website's old search button menu function was deleted as it was obsolete for website usage and did not return accurate results. By replacing this with a donate button, attention is called to the most important thing users can do viewing the site: donate to the foundation.



...Allignment



I changed the alignment of elements to draw attention back to the text and create a flow between the content. All elements are now centered or right aligned rather than shifting back and forth at random.



...Photo Orientation and Replacement



I changed the direction of the photos to face the text because of the psychology of design. When users view a page, they tend to follow the eye line of the subject in the photo.
